Sourcing

We carefully select our raw materials by having qualified technical staff on ground to check if the commodity fits the international quality specifications. We look at physical attri- butes of the commodity (color, foreign matter, grain size, maturity, smell, etc.) and take samples to certified labs to check for presence of organisms e.g. aflatoxins that could be harmful to both human and animal life. It is upon confirma- tion by the sourcing team that the product can be transport- ed to processing facility or market.

Processing & Production

We boast of (with) state of the art modern machinery which we use to process (clean, dry, sort, mill) Once the raw materials arrive at the production plant, our QCM on ground checks the quality again to ensure nothing was over looked during sourcing. Upon confirmation and quality and quantity then processing and production commence. We have a drying and cleaning capacity of 200 tons per day and a milling capacity of 40 tons per day.
